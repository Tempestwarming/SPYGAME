FIND THE SPY

RULES

1. Pass the phone. Each player swipes up to see the word, except the Spy.

2. Ask vague questions or give one-word clues about the secret word.

3. The Spy must blend in and guess the word from clues.

4. Discuss and vote. Find the Spy before time runs out!
