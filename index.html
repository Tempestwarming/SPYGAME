HOW TO PLAY

1. Pass the phone to Player 1.

2. Swipe up on the screen to reveal if you are a Citizen or the Spy.

3. Citizens will see a Secret Word. The Spy sees nothing!

4. Take turns asking questions to find out who doesn't know the word.

5. The Spy must try to blend in and guess the word.
